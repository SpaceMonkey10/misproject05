My Hobbies & Interests

Rock Climbing

I started rock climbing when I transferred to UMD last year and joined the rock climbing team! It is the perfect sport for me as it mixes intensity, endurance, explosiveness, and the chance to be outside. I have found a great community of climbers here at UMD, and it really helped me get acclimated to the school and make my first group of friends.

Further, rock climbing introduced me to my current roommates, which opened a wide range of climbing opportunities outdoors. I am currently a captain on the rock climbing team, which has been a great experience so far!

Fishing

I have been fishing ever since my dad introduced me to it as a boy. Before I could drive, I would bike to my local lakes with two fishing rods in my baseball bag and spend all day fishing. Once or twice a summer, my family would drive up north to the Hayward area to fish, swim, tube, and enjoy nature (but mostly fish).

I have continued to fish here in Duluth in local lakes like Fish Lake and Pike Lake, though Lake Superior is a bit much for my small fishing boat to handle.

Hiking

Another outdoor activity I love is hiking. Walking through a remote forest always makes me feel so in touch with nature and gives me a sense of relaxation similar to that of fishing. Hiking, however, is far more physically involved than fishing, so I can reap the benefits of exercise and time in nature.

Duluth is the perfect place for hiking, whether it be on the superior hiking trail, or walking by the lake, there are plenty of options.
