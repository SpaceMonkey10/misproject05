Discover UMD

Your Reasons for Choosing University of Minnesota Duluth

I chose UMD because of the countless outdoor activities in proximity to campus, so I could go to school with my best friend, and so I could be closer to home.

Things You Enjoy About the City and Area

I love living so close to Lake Superior. Swimming in the lake during the summer is so fun! Canal Park and the West End of Duluth are also personal highlights. Specifically, I really like all the coffee shops in Duluth; there is healthy competition that pushes each business to do better.

UMD Marketing Video

Campus Experiences

When I transferred to UMD, I initially regretted my decision. Thankfully, my experiences here have convinced me otherwise. Joining the UMD Climbing Team has provided me with a great sense of community and allowed me to form connections both in and out of school. I have also participated in outdoor climbing trips, which initially got me interested in the Climbing Team.
